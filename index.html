About

We recognize the hardships that the hispanic youth community faces when it comes to mental health. There is a lack of resources for Hispanics in general and a narrative about mental within this community. Such as mental health issues get seen as a myth and are not real in the eyes of many which is very untrue and makes it hard for the youth growing up under these stigmas to seek out the help or resources. This is what Mente Positiva hopes to bring, awareness about mental health. This is to celebrate empowerment to our youth who is the future and rebuild a community that values well-being and fostering space spaces and educating them on how to seek the resources they need.

Schedule


Day 1


Scanvenger Hunt

12pm - 6pm

Upon arrival there is an information/welcome booth where you are able to pick up a map that shows the different information booths we have at the festival. Go around and learn about the inportance of mental health and learn more about what resources are there for you, but don't forget to get your map stamped for a prize after you have visited all booths on your map.


Talk Panel

3pm - 4:30pm

Join us for our first talk panel which 3 mental health providers will be beginning our discussion. Topics covered will include what is mental health, how to notice signs, how to seek help, and how we can support others.


6pm - 7:30pm

Our second panel will be joined by Selena Gomez who is an active mental health advocate and will share her personal story about how she has navigated through her struggle of dealing with depression and bipolar disorder


Day 2


Car Show


Our day long car show will accompany Mente Positiva and showcase beautiful vibrant cars, bringing in a car loving community and a community in support of Hispanic youth mental health


Mercado


Supporting our car show, a mercado will also take place. Our mercado will showcase local hispanic vendors who will offer a multitude of things


Day 3


Bailes

Every 2 hours

Repeating every 2 hours, dancers will perform the traditional Jarabe Tapatio around the festival


Concert

6:30pm - 8pm

Closing out Mente Positiva, Kali Uchis will take the main stage
